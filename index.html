About “Committed with Devin Greer”

Hi beautiful people! My name is Devin, and I’m a women’s soccer player at UCSB. I’m launching a multimedia podcast that amplifies the voices of underrepresented students, with a focus on Black female college athletes. This podcast tackles barriers in pay-to-play sports and the complicated college application process by offering free, accessible guidance on recruiting, navigating college athletics, and choosing the right college. This podcast and website is a one stop place to provide guidance and empower Black students and other minority communities to pursue higher education and their passions—whether in athletics, the arts, science, writing, or beyond.

About the Podcast

Committed is a multimedia podcast and resource hub dedicated to empowering Black students and other marginalized communities to pursue higher education. We break down barriers in pay‑to‑play sports, demystify the college application process, and highlight the stories of Black athletes who are thriving in predominantly white institutions.
