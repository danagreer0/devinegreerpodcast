About the Podcast

Committed is a multimedia podcast and resource hub dedicated to empowering Black students and other marginalized communities to pursue higher education. We break down barriers in pay‑to‑play sports, demystify the college application process, and highlight the stories of Black athletes who are thriving in predominantly white institutions.
